# Business Model Canvas for Silicon-Infer

## Customer Segments
- Developers using high-end Apple Silicon hardware.
- Tech startups focusing on AI and machine learning applications.
- Enterprises requiring fast local inference for coding tasks.
- Educational institutions teaching AI and programming on Apple devices.

## Value Propositions
- High-throughput inference capabilities (>200 t/s) optimized for Apple Silicon.
- Affordable local coding models reducing reliance on cloud services.
- Enhanced reasoning and tool-calling capabilities for improved developer productivity.
- Easy integration with existing development environments and workflows.

## Channels
- Direct sales through the official website and GitHub repository.
- Partnerships with developer-focused platforms (e.g., GitHub Marketplace).
- Online tech forums and communities (e.g., Reddit, Stack Overflow).
- Developer conferences and workshops for demonstrations and training.

## Customer Relationships
- Community support via forums and GitHub issues.
- Regular updates and feature enhancements based on user feedback.
- Dedicated customer support for enterprise clients.
- Online tutorials and documentation to facilitate onboarding.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model: $29/month (approx. 1,000 THB) for individual developers.
- Enterprise licensing: $499/month (approx. 17,500 THB) for teams.
- Consulting services for custom integrations and optimizations.
- Training and workshops for organizations adopting the technology.

## Key Resources
- Development team with expertise in AI and Apple Silicon optimization.
- GitHub repository for version control and community engagement.
- Marketing resources for outreach and promotion.
- Infrastructure for hosting and distributing the software.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and optimization of the coding model.
- Marketing and outreach to attract developers and enterprises.
- Community engagement through forums and social media.
- Customer support and training to ensure successful adoption.

## Key Partners
- Apple for potential collaboration and optimization insights.
- Developer communities and forums for outreach and feedback.
- Educational institutions for partnerships in teaching and research.
- Cloud service providers for hybrid deployment options.

## Cost Structure
- Development costs: salaries, tools, and technologies (~$150,000/year or ~5,250,000 THB).
- Marketing expenses: online ads, conference participation (~$30,000/year or ~1,050,000 THB).
- Customer support costs: staffing and resources (~$20,000/year or ~700,000 THB).
- Infrastructure costs: hosting and distribution (~$10,000/year or ~350,000 THB).
